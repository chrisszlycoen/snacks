Food and Drinks

Chicken Burger

Chicken Burger is a popular fast food item that typically consists of a bread bun, a patty of grilled or fried chicken, and various toppings and sauces. Sales have increased by 25% in the past year, with prices ranging from $8-12 at most restaurants. It is a versatile and delicious choice for those who enjoy a quick and satisfying meal.

Chicken Panang Curry

Chicken Panang Curry is a rich Thai dish featuring tender chicken in a creamy coconut curry sauce with aromatic spices. Its popularity has grown 35% in the last year, with the average restaurant price ranging from $14-18 per serving.

Iced hazelnut Coffee

Iced hazelnut coffee is a trendy cold coffee drink featuring sweet hazelnut syrup and chilled espresso over ice. This refreshing beverage has seen a massive surge in popularity, especially among young coffee lovers, with sales increasing over 40% in the past two years as more cafes add it to their permanent menus. The average price ranges from $4.50-6.00 depending on size and location.
